Surfs Up!

Brady is a 17 year old young man, he has a burning passion for surfing, especially if it is with his girlfriend Mac. Brady has this idea of the perfect summer where him and Mac get to surf all day everyday, watching his favourite movie Wet Side Story in Macks grandfathers surf shop Big Poppa's. The shop is located beach front in California, USA. Having immideiate access to the beach drives Brady's love for surfing and how he found the love of his life.

The two of them have no idea what kind of summer they are about to experiance. They soonlearned the horrible news that Macks aunt was going to take her away to boarding school in the fall,which meant that theyre time together was coming to an end very quickly. Although Mack did not want to let the news put a damper on their summer, Brady could not help but worry about what would happen to their relationship.
