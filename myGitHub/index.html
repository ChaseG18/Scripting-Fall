Get To Know Chase

Chase is a first year Multimedia and Production Diploma student at the Lethbridge College. In his spare time, he loves to play Minecraft with his friends. He loves to create servers to share and play on with his friends and because of the ability to refine his skills in Adobe Dreamweaver, he uses his skill to now create texture packs and mod packs for the game. He plans to use the skills that he is gaining to further a career in photography, video game development and web design. Chase also works with The Skipping Stones Foundation which is a non-profit organization that is based in Calgary, Alberta. He is working with them to try and improve the medical field for the transgender community in Southern Alberta. Chase also started the first ever Gay Straight Alliance (GSA) club at his high school in his grade 11 year, growing up in a very conservative farmer/rancher community, he was faced with a lot of diversity before reaching his end goal. Outside of school you can find Chase gaming on his PC, spending time with his friends and family or going for walks in nature with his adorable fur baby Rallo. The person that inspires Chase the most is his mother, Michelle. His mother has been his biggest motivator and inspiration for everything that Chase has done. Despite coming from a broken home and being raised by a single mother, Chase uses that turmoil as fuel to achieve his goals.

Chase has played this game since 2016, and has continue to advance his knowledge of the game. He has begun to tinker with the creation of mod packs and texture packs for the game and plans to continue down that road in the future.

Fortnite is a combate, online game that allows 100 players into a lobby to battle till there is one man or one team left standing. By searching through chests and lott boxes/crates you can find better weapons to help defend yourself better.

Phasmophobia is a horror related game that can be played amongest multiple different plateforms. Chase has only recently began playing and streaming this game and many views and followers have taken a liking to watching him play this spooky game.
