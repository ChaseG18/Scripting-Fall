Introduction

“The Greenhouse Capital of the Prairies” home to 5,500+ people, this small town is perfect for starter families or those looking for that small town feel but still be a short drive into the city. The Town of Redcliff holds so many opportunities for everyone who calls it home. With only being fifteen minutes from Medicine Hat, one hour from Brooks, or 3 hours from Calgary. There is no lost opportunity for work, shopping, recreation and unique cuisine. If you happen to stroll down the red trail just on outside the golf course, keep an eye out to see if you can find the hidden secrets and paths that will take you deeper into the coulees. Some have said that it is almost like going to a different planet. Finding the magnificent red cliffs that stretch as far as the eye can see, and the clay pits that almost make you feel like you are walking on the moon. With the light grey dried clay, with grooves that tell its story of where it originally came from. Curves that dance around the hills and rocks, creating patterns and a hundred different textures. Every once and a while you may find a sinkhole, falling so deep that they look like they may go down to the core of the Earth. Caused by coal mines that were used in the 1880’s, collapsing after all the years of erosion and rainfall. From the outside, Redcliff seems like it would be a place you would move after retirement. But, with an open mind and a sense of wonder, you can find the true beauty that hides within.
