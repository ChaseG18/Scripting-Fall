Economy Within Redcliff and Medicine Hat

Since Redcliff boarders Medicine Hat, many people commute to the city for work. The short drive makes it easy to widen the occupational options, attracts a more diverse group of people and enhances the quality of life for the towns people. 25.3% of Redcliff residence work in the sales and service industry and 22.9% work with the trades and transport industry. Walking up town Redcliff you will see dozens of self-sustaining businesses, from bakeries and comfort food to jewelry and knick knacks. The people in this town make sure that their customers have everything they could ever need, and leave happier than they came in.

Local Agriculture

The main source of income in this community would be from the greenhouses and agriculture. Seeing as Redcliff is the “Greenhouse Capital of The Prairies” this industry supplies many residents with jobs. Many residents within the town come from all over the world to work in these greenhouses. The farms and greenhouses are what supply the town with most of their fresh fruits and vegetables as well as grains and meat products.
